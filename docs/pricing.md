## FAQ

### Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount. When downgrading, you'll receive credit toward future billing.

### What happens if I exceed my event limit?
On the Free plan, events beyond the daily limit are dropped. On Pro, you'll be charged $0.001 per additional event. Enterprise plans have no limits.

### Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes, every new account gets a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan with full features.

### Do you offer discounts for startups?
Yes! If you're backed by Y Combinator, Techstars, or similar accelerators, you qualify for 50% off Pro for the first year. Contact sales@acme-analytics.com.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex) and wire transfer for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

### Can I get a refund?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.
